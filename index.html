Who we are

We are a group of young neuroscientists who are passionately dedicated to helping the next generation of neuroscientists, hoping to achieve a better understanding of neuroscience through our events, websites, sessions, and research papers.

Our Vision

1. Well rounded about neuroscience and wrote issues about it.
2. Well prepared for international competitions such as BB.
3. Well-known about the careers available with a neuroscience degree.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ignite curiosity about neuroscience among high school students in our school and around the globe. We provide guidance, resources, and an atmosphere to engage in meaningful neuroscience endeavors — promoting innovation, critical thinking, and self-development.
